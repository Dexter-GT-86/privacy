DexDirect Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

DexDirect (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the DexDirect mobile application. This page informs users about how we collect, use, and protect personal information when using our app.

Information We Collect

DexDirect may collect the following information when you use the app:
	•	Name
	•	Email address
	•	Account login information
	•	Appointment and clinic interaction data
	•	Basic device and usage information

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
	•	Provide and operate the app
	•	Enable appointment booking and clinic connectivity
	•	Improve app performance and reliability
	•	Provide customer support
	•	Maintain security and prevent misuse

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data. All sensitive data is transmitted using secure encryption.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except:
	•	When required by law
	•	To provide core app services (such as secure cloud hosting)

User Rights

Users may:
	•	Request access to their data
	•	Request deletion of their account and data
	•	Contact us for privacy concerns

To request deletion, contact: support@dexdirect.com

Children’s Privacy

DexDirect is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
